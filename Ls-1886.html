LS-R700

DP Leak Testers

Entry-level model of Air Leak Testers that has the same high detection capability as higher-end models.

Good cost performance realized by integrating the necessary functions into a compact body.

Specs

Range: -100 kPa to 1.5 MPa

source: 100 to 240 VAC, 50/60 Hz, 70 VA max

Operating temperature: 5 to 40℃

Storage temperature:-20 to 70℃

External dimensions: W165 x D240 x H208 (excluding protrusions)

Weight: Approx. 6.3 kg (Standard spec.)


Features

Highly sensitive and pressure-resistant DPS. Air operated valves that are not affected by heat.

The Intelligent Pneumatic Circuit with excellent performance and safety checks valve operation and sensor sensitivity in every test.

Master Preset and NR Mode can improve detection and reduce test time.

Extensive channel functions for a wide variety of works.
