Who We Are

Headquartered in Gurugram, Cosmo India has grown into a full-scale engineering company with a skilled team capable of designing and manufacturing customized testing systems tailored to diverse industrial applications.

Our comprehensive product range — advanced air leak testers and flow testers — is engineered to ensure the highest standards of quality control across industries.

Beyond manufacturing, we offer end-to-end support including installation, calibration, and after-sales service — ensuring seamless operation and long-term performance for every client we serve.
