What is a Flow Meter？

A flowmeter is an automatic leak tester that measures gases flowing through piping, etc. or that makes a pass/fail judgment by measuring the flow of gases caused by leaks in parts or finished products that require airtightness. Typical sensors include volumetric flowmeters using laminar flow tubes and mass flowmeters using mass flow sensors.
