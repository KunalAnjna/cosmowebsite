About us

Cosmo Instruments India Pvt. Ltd. has established a strong presence in India as a trusted provider of air leak and flow testing solutions. Since beginning its operations in 2007 from Gurugram, the company has expanded its footprint across key industrial regions including Manesar, Bengaluru, Pune, Chennai, and Gujarat, with dedicated sales, service, and manufacturing facilities.

Over the years, Cosmo India has developed a skilled team of engineers capable of delivering customized testing solutions to meet diverse industry requirements.

With strong in-house manufacturing capabilities, Cosmo India designs and builds leak testing systems tailored to specific applications. Along with manufacturing, the company provides end-to-end support including installation, calibration, and after-sales service.

Focused on quality, technical expertise, and customer satisfaction, Cosmo Instruments India continues to support industries with reliable solutions and consistent service across the country.
