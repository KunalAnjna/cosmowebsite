What is a Cosmo Super Gel？

Cosmo Super Gel is a resin with extremely low hardness. Excellent in stretch and durability, it is very much similar to a liquid and maintains its shape after being formed. It realizes air leak testing of works with shapes that were previously difficult to seal.
