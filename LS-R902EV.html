LS-R902EV

EV Leak Testers

Differential pressure Air Leak Tester that can perform highly sensitive testing under an environment of ultra-high pressure.

It realizes differential pressure leak test with a resolution of 1 Pa at up to 16 MPa..

Specs

Range: 10 MPa, 16 MPa

source:100 to 240 VAC, 50/60 Hz, 70 VA max

Operating temperature: 5 to 40℃

Storage temperature:-20 to 60℃

External dimensions: W82 x D230 x H198 (Pneumatic Unit)

Weight:Controller: Approx. 2.6 kg Pneumatic Unit (LUA-241): Approx. 15 kg


Features

Monitoring of test pressure and differential pressure (leak) waveforms.

Compensates for noise effects on the works and reduces test time

Multi-language (Japanese, English, Chinese)..

Sensor unit can be placed close to the work.
